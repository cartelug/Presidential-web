Sources

Nothing on this site is unsourced. Each bracketed number — like [4] — points to the publication below.

  1. 1Uganda National Roads Authority / Ministry of Works & Transport — national paved road network statistics.
  2. 2Electoral Commission of Uganda — official count of districts and cities.
  3. 3Kampala–Entebbe Expressway (KEE) — route length, opening date and journey-time figures.
  4. 4Uganda Bureau of Statistics (UBOS) / World Bank — GDP per capita, literacy and national poverty headcount.
  5. 5Office of the Prime Minister / UNHCR Uganda — refugee hosting figures under the Refugee Act, 2006.
  6. 6Electricity Regulatory Authority / UETCL — installed generation capacity.
  7. 7Public reporting on the Source of the Nile Bridge, Jinja — span length and commissioning date.

Figures are stated against the most recent published data at time of writing. Where estimates vary between publications, the more conservative figure is used.
